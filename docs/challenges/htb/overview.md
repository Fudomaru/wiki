# Hack The Box

HackTheBox is where I take on real lab machines in an isolated environment.
Each box is a small world of its own —
a misconfigured service, a forgotten credential, a logic flaw waiting to be pulled apart.

The write-ups here follow the machine from first scan to root.
No spoilers skipped, no lucky guesses glossed over.
If something took me an embarrassing amount of time, that stays in.
The struggle is the point.
